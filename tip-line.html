Tip Line

Have a document, a leak, or a story that needs to be told? We want to hear from you.

We Protect Our Sources

If you have information about government misconduct, corporate fraud, surveillance programs, financial corruption, or any story the powerful don't want told — we want to hear from you. We take source protection seriously and follow established secure journalism practices.

Do not contact us through social media, standard email, or any platform you don't control for sensitive submissions. Use the secure channels below.

Secure Submission Options

Most secure: Protonmail
Send encrypted email to: tips-newsanarchist@proton.me
If you don't have a Protonmail account, create one free at proton.me before contacting us. Do not use your real name or identifying information when creating the account.

You will receive an automated confirmation reply. We review all submissions and will contact you via Protonmail if we need more information. We do not use Signal at this time.

Standard (non-sensitive tips only)

For non-sensitive story tips, press releases, and general leads:
editorial@newsanarchist.com

What to Send

  • Government documents, internal memos, FOIA documents, court filings
  • Corporate internal communications, financial records, compliance reports
  • Whistleblower accounts with supporting documentation
  • Surveillance evidence, data collection disclosures, law enforcement records
  • Any primary-source material relating to our coverage areas

What Happens After You Submit

Every submission is reviewed by our editorial team. We will contact you via the same secure channel you used to reach us. We do not publish identifying information about sources without explicit consent. We will tell you before we publish anything based on your submission and give you an opportunity to review how your information is characterized.

We cannot guarantee publication — we evaluate every tip on its newsworthiness, verifiability, and public interest value. But we read everything.

Source Protection Policy

  • We do not store identifying metadata from secure submissions on internet-connected systems.
  • We do not share source information with third parties under any circumstances, including law enforcement, without a valid court order.
  • If we receive a legal demand for source information, we will notify the source to the extent legally permitted and will contest the demand.
  • We recommend sources review the Freedom of the Press Foundation's security training before making sensitive submissions.

Our Coverage Areas

We are especially interested in tips relating to: government surveillance programs, FOIA-responsive documents, corporate fraud and antitrust violations, financial market manipulation, intelligence community activities, unexplained phenomena and UAP-related government programs, wrongful convictions and prosecutorial misconduct, and geopolitical operations not covered by mainstream media.

Questions

Questions about the tip line or our source protection practices: editorial@newsanarchist.com
