About Our Authors

A note on NewsAnarchist's editorial identities

NewsAnarchist publishes investigative news and analysis under named editorial personas. Each author represents a distinct beat, credential background, and editorial voice. In the tradition of pseudonymous publishing — from Benjamin Franklin's "Silence Dogood" to contemporary AI-assisted journalism — our authors are editorial identities operating under human editorial oversight.

All content is reviewed for accuracy, sourcing, and editorial standards before publication. Primary sources are linked where available. Corrections are issued promptly.
