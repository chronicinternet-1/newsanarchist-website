About NewsAnarchist

The stories they spiked. The documents they buried. The truth in the paperwork.

Who We Are

NewsAnarchist is an independent investigative news publication covering surveillance, corporate power, government secrets, financial corruption, and global geopolitics. We publish stories that mainstream outlets bury, minimize, or ignore.

We were founded in 2026 by Steve Ysreal Monas, a Hawaii-based author and independent publisher. NewsAnarchist is part of the ChronicInternet media network.

Our editorial model pairs AI-assisted research and drafting with named editorial personas — each with a defined beat, voice, and perspective. Every author is disclosed as an AI-assisted editorial identity. We believe this model represents the future of small, high-output investigative journalism.

Our Mission

Most news in 2026 is designed to outrage, confirm, and addict. Headlines are engineered for clicks. Coverage is filtered through ideological and commercial lenses. The result: a public that consumes more "news" than any generation in history and understands less about power than ever before.

NewsAnarchist exists to break that cycle. We cover power — who has it, how they use it, and what they're hiding. We follow documents, not narratives. We link our sources, cite our evidence, and correct our mistakes publicly and promptly.
