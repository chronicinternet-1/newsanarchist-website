"We don't tell you what to think. We make sure you have what you need to think for yourself."

Who We Are

NewsAnarchist was built on a simple, radical premise: the news should be fast, factual, and free of agenda. We are an AI-powered, human-curated news platform that publishes 20–30 articles every day across politics, technology, world affairs, culture, and opinion.

We are not affiliated with any political party, ideology, media conglomerate, or government. We have no investors to appease, no advertisers to protect (yet), and no partisan agenda to advance. We follow the facts — wherever they lead.

Our Mission

Most news in 2026 is designed to outrage, addict, and confirm what you already believe. Headlines are engineered for clicks. Coverage is filtered through ideological lenses. The result: a population that consumes more "news" than any generation in history and understands less.

NewsAnarchist exists to break that cycle. We believe an informed citizenry is the foundation of a functioning democracy — and that right now, the information environment is failing that citizenry badly.

Our mission: deliver the most important stories, as fast as possible, as accurately as possible, without the noise.

Editorial Voice

We take inspiration from CitizenFreePress's headline-first intensity and The Free Press's commitment to original, quality journalism. The result is something new: high-volume, SEO-driven, AI-assisted reporting that prioritizes clarity over commentary.

  • No political bias — We cover all sides. Left, right, center. The story determines the angle, not our priors.
  • Fact-first — No speculation without disclosure. No anonymous sourcing without strong editorial justification.
  • Headline-driven — Bold, clear, and clickable — but not clickbait. The headline must deliver on its promise.
  • Transparency — AI-generated or AI-assisted content is disclosed in every byline. We believe transparency builds trust.
  • Corrections — When we get it wrong, we say so clearly, quickly, and prominently.
  • No sensationalism — Report facts. Avoid fear-mongering. Let readers decide.

How We Work

NewsAnarchist runs an automated content pipeline built on Claude AI (Anthropic) and a suite of custom agents developed by our founding team. The pipeline:

  • Monitors Google Trends, Reddit, and major news feeds hourly
  • Identifies the highest-signal, most-searched topics of the moment
  • Generates fact-checked, multi-source articles using AI — reviewed and approved by human editors
  • Publishes to Cloudflare's global edge network within minutes of breaking events

The goal is the responsiveness of a wire service with the depth of a long-form outlet. We're not there yet. But we're building toward it, one article at a time.

AI Disclosure Policy

We believe AI disclosure is non-negotiable. Every article on NewsAnarchist includes one of the following bylines:

  • "NewsAnarchist Team" — Human-written and edited, no AI involvement
  • "NewsAnarchist Team (AI-Assisted)" — AI-drafted with human review and editing
  • "NewsAnarchist AI" — AI-generated, reviewed for accuracy by editors

We verify all factual claims against at least three independent sources. We do not publish AI-generated content on breaking news until the core facts have been confirmed by established outlets.

Our Business Model

NewsAnarchist is free to read. We believe paywalls on news are fundamentally anti-democratic. Our revenue comes from:

  • Display advertising — Standard web ads, introduced once we reach scale
  • Premium subscriptions ($5/month) — Ad-free reading, exclusive deep-dives, early breaking news access
  • Pro subscriptions ($15/month) — All Premium features plus member newsletters and live Q&As

We are built on Cloudflare's free-tier infrastructure, which means near-zero operating costs and 100% uptime. When we make money, it funds better journalism — not VC returns.
