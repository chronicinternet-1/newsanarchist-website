About NewsAnarchist

Independent investigative news · Kapa'a, Hawaii

NewsAnarchist is an independent investigative news publication covering surveillance, corporate power, government secrets, technology and privacy, global power, markets, unexplained phenomena, and true crime. We publish the stories buried, spiked, or spun by the mainstream press.

Our Mission

We believe in radical transparency, primary-source journalism, and editorial independence. We have no corporate owners, no political party affiliations, and no advertiser influence over our editorial decisions. Our revenue comes from reader subscriptions and contextual advertising.

Our Editorial Model

NewsAnarchist uses AI language models as research and drafting assistants under human editorial oversight. All published content is reviewed for accuracy, sourcing, and editorial standards before publication. AI assistance is disclosed in every byline. We do not publish unreviewed AI output.

Our Reporters

Our editorial team consists of six named personas, each with a specific beat and editorial voice. See About Our Authors for full bios and disclosures.

Publisher

NewsAnarchist is published by Steve Ysreal Monas, a solo founder and author based in Kapa'a, Hawaii. Steve is also the publisher of stevemonas.com and founder of Chronic Internet.
