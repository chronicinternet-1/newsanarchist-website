US Unleashes Another Wave Of Crude From Strategic Reserve As Gas Prices Soar

What they're not telling you: # US Unleashes Another Wave Of Crude From Strategic Reserve As Gas Prices Soar The United States is systematically depleting its Strategic Petroleum Reserve by loaning crude directly to oil corporations as prices remain elevated, revealing how government energy policy prioritizes corporate supply chains over genuine energy independence. The Department of Energy announced it would loan 53 million barrels of oil from the SPR to petroleum companies, marking another massive drawdown from the nation's emergency reserves. Deliveries began immediately from storage sites in Louisiana and Texas.

The official rationale centers on stabilizing oil prices amid geopolitical tensions.
