The incident was reported to the Pentagon's All-Domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO), which investigates reports of unexplained objects.

The release of these files is the third tranche of documents made public by the Trump administration, with the first two releases occurring in 2021 and 2022. The latest release adds dozens of new documents, photographs, and videos to the government's archive of unexplained sightings.
