The real shift shouldn't be "how I see privacy." It's recognizing the game is rigged. Healthcare providers aren't security-first organizations; they're data-first organizations with compliance departments. That health service partnering with Du? They've already calculated liability against profit. Your contractual privacy promises mean nothing against a determined adversary. Gijs now "sees" privacy differently. But what's he actually doing?
