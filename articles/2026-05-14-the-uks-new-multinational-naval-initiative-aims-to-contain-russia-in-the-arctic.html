General Sir Gwyn Jenkins, head of the British Royal Navy, announced the formation of the Northern Navies Initiative (NNI) with counterparts from the UK, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, and the Netherlands.
