"But we’re ready for any compromises, except those involving our dignity and sovereignty," he added.

The West, or rather the Roman Catholic and Protestant churches, will celebrate on April 5. While full ceasefires, even short ones, have not had much success in the past more than four years of war, the two sides have previously agreed to days or even weeks of pauses on attacking energy sites.
