Government Benefits Fraud & Financial Crime
Jordan Ames
Former federal fraud investigator. 9 years tracking benefits fraud, wire fraud, and financial crime networks.

Jordan Ames spent nearly a decade inside federal fraud investigations before concluding that the system was better at generating press releases than prosecutions. His reporting follows the money — from ghost hospice providers billing Medicare for patients who never existed, to SNAP fraud rings operating across state lines, to the shell companies that make it all disappear. Ames believes fraud at this scale doesn't happen by accident: it requires negligent oversight, captured regulators, and politicians who benefit from looking the other way. He documents all three.
