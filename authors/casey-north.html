Casey North

Science journalist. Former aerospace industry analyst.

Casey North covers the territory where credentialed skepticism meets documented anomaly. That means UAP congressional hearings, government non-disclosure agreements with aerospace contractors, near-death experience research at major universities, and emerging technologies whose capabilities are classified while their existence is public. North does not believe everything — but takes seriously the documented evidence that official explanations sometimes require more faith than the phenomena they dismiss. The approach is Debrief-style: rigorous, sourced, and unwilling to mock what the documents actually show.
